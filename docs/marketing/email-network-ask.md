# Personal network email — warm intro ask

**Goal:** ask 10 people you already know if they know any RCFE admins to introduce you to. Warm intros convert 5–10× cold outreach.

**Who to send to:**
- Anyone in healthcare you've ever worked with
- Geriatric care managers / placement consultants you know
- Doctors or nurses in your network
- Friends / family with parents in care
- Anyone who has visited a residential care home for any reason
- Former colleagues now in healthcare ops, even tangentially
- Your own primary-care doctor (they may know placement consultants)

**Rules:**
1. Three sentences max. Long emails get ignored.
2. The ask is **not** "buy this" — it's "do you know anyone I could talk to?" People hate being sold to; they love being consulted.
3. Make it easy to say no. End with "no worries if not."
4. Send 10 of these tomorrow morning. Total time: 10 minutes.

## Template

**Subject:** Quick ask — do you know anyone running a small residential care home?

> Hi {{name}},
>
> I've spent the last year building Kinroster, a tool that takes the documentation tax off small residential care facilities (6–20 beds) — caregivers speak a 90-second shift note, AI structures it, families and doctors get the right info without the admin chasing it down. It's live in two regions now and I'm looking for 5 pilot facilities.
>
> Long shot, but do you know anyone running or working at a small RCFE / assisted living home I could chat with? Even a 15-min "is this useful?" call would help me a lot. No worries if not — and happy to share what I learn either way.
>
> Thanks {{name}}.
>
> — Pouya

## After they reply

If they say **yes / I know someone:**
> Amazing — could you forward this email to them, or introduce us via a separate thread? No pressure if it's awkward; happy to draft an intro you can paste.

If they say **no / I don't know anyone but I'm curious:**
> All good. If you want to take a quick look at what I built, kinroster.com. And if anyone ever comes to mind, my inbox is always open.

If they don't reply: **don't follow up.** This is your personal network — don't burn the relationship over a low-stakes ask.

---

## Tracking

Same Notion / spreadsheet as the LinkedIn outreach. Columns:
- Name
- Relationship to you
- Date sent
- Reply (Y / N)
- Intro made (Y / N)
- Who they introduced you to

After two weeks, count: how many warm intros did this generate? If <3, you may need to widen the network or reframe the ask. If ≥5, double down — this is your highest-leverage channel.
