## 1. Purpose

Kinroster is an AI-assisted documentation tool for residential care facilities. This agreement establishes the terms under which your facility will participate in the Kinroster pilot program as a design partner.

---

## 2. What You Receive

As a pilot design partner, you receive:

- **Free lifetime access** to Kinroster (no monthly fee, for as long as Kinroster operates)
- Unlimited users and residents on the platform
- Priority access to new features
- Direct communication with the Kinroster team for support and feedback

---

## 3. What You Commit To

As a pilot design partner, you agree to:

| Commitment | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Use Kinroster for documentation** | Use Kinroster for all shift notes for at least 4 residents for the duration of the pilot |
| **Weekly feedback calls** | Participate in a 15-minute phone or video call each week for the first 8 weeks to share feedback |
| **Allow anonymized usage data** | Permit Kinroster to analyze usage patterns (number of notes, feature usage, error rates) in anonymized form to improve the product |
| **Provide a testimonial** | If satisfied with Kinroster, provide a written testimonial or case study that Kinroster may use for marketing (you will review and approve before publication) |

## 4. AI and Data Handling Acknowledgment

By signing this agreement, you confirm that you have read and understood the **Kinroster Data Handling Disclosure** (provided separately) and that you acknowledge the following:

### 4a. AI Usage

- [ ] I understand that Kinroster uses artificial intelligence (Anthropic Claude) to structure caregiver notes, detect potential incidents, generate family email drafts, and compile weekly care summaries.
- [ ] I understand that AI-generated content is always reviewed by a human (caregiver or admin) before it becomes part of the official record or is sent to anyone.
- [ ] I understand that the AI does not make medical diagnoses, recommend treatments, or make clinical decisions.
- [ ] I understand that the AI may occasionally produce inaccurate output, and that my staff is responsible for reviewing and correcting AI-generated content before saving.

### 4b. Data Processing

- [ ] I understand that resident names, conditions, and caregiver notes are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI service for processing, and that this data is not retained by Anthropic beyond 30 days.
- [ ] I understand that if voice input is used, audio recordings are sent to a transcription service and immediately discarded after transcription. Audio is never stored.
- [ ] I understand that all facility data is stored in an encrypted database in the United States.
- [ ] I understand that Kinroster does not sell, share, or provide my facility's data to insurance companies, pharmaceutical companies, data brokers, or any third party beyond what is described in the Data Handling Disclosure.

### 4c. Compliance Status

- [ ] I understand that Kinroster is pursuing but has not yet completed formal HIPAA compliance, including Business Associate Agreements with all subprocessors.
- [ ] I understand that Kinroster has implemented technical safeguards (encryption, access controls, data isolation) as described in the Data Handling Disclosure.
- [ ] I accept Kinroster's current compliance status as described in the Data Handling Disclosure and understand that Kinroster is actively working toward full HIPAA compliance.
- [ ] I understand that I am responsible for informing my residents (or their legal representatives) that AI-assisted documentation tools are being used at my facility.

## 5. Resident Notification

You agree to inform your residents (or their authorized representatives) that your facility uses AI-assisted documentation tools. At minimum, this notification should include:

1. That caregiver notes are processed by AI to create structured documentation
2. That resident names and health observations are processed by the AI service
3. That AI-generated content is always reviewed by staff before it becomes part of the record
4. That no audio recordings are stored
5. That residents or their representatives may contact you with questions or concerns

Kinroster can provide a template notification letter for your use if needed.

## 6. Confidentiality

### Kinroster's Obligations

- We will not disclose your facility's data to any third party except as described in the Data Handling Disclosure
- We will not use your facility's identifiable data for marketing without your written approval
- We will not identify your facility by name in any public materials without your written approval
- Anonymized, aggregated usage data (e.g., "pilot facilities entered an average of 35 notes per day") may be used without identifying your facility

### Your Obligations

- You agree to keep confidential any non-public features, roadmap information, or pricing details shared with you during the pilot program
- This does not prevent you from discussing your general experience with Kinroster or referring other facility operators

## 7. Data Ownership

- **Your data belongs to you.** Kinroster does not claim ownership of any data you or your staff enter into the system.
- You may request a complete export of your data at any time. Kinroster will provide the export within 5 business days.
- If you stop using Kinroster, your data will be retained for the legally required period (per your state's regulations) and then deleted upon your request.

## 8. Limitation of Liability

- Kinroster is provided "as is" during the pilot period. We make no guarantees of uptime, availability, or AI accuracy.
- Kinroster is a documentation tool, not a clinical decision support system. It does not replace clinical judgment, state-required documentation practices, or professional care standards.
- You remain solely responsible for the accuracy of your facility's official records, compliance with state regulations, and the quality of care provided to your residents.
- Kinroster's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the fees paid (which, for pilot partners, is $0).

---

## 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with 14 days written notice.
- If you terminate, your free lifetime access is revoked. Your data remains available for export for 90 days after termination.
- If Kinroster ceases operations, we will provide at least 30 days notice and make all your data available for export.
